Flood Safety Tips

  • 📢 Stay updated: Follow weather alerts and local news for flood warnings.
  • 🛠 Prepare an emergency kit: Include essentials like food, water, flashlight, and first-aid supplies.
  • 🚫 Avoid floodwaters: Even shallow water can be dangerous.
  • 🏔 Move to higher ground: If flooding is expected, relocate to a safer area.
  • ⚡️ Turn off power: If necessary, switch off electricity and gas to prevent hazards.
  • 🚨 Follow evacuation orders: Leave immediately if advised by authorities.
  • 📱 Keep communication ready: Have a charged phone and emergency contacts saved.
  • 💧 Store clean water: Fill bottles and containers in case of water supply issues.
  • 🏥 Know emergency shelters: Identify nearby safe locations in advance.
