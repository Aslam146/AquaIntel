First Aid Measures for Flood Situations

  • Avoid Contaminated Water: Floodwaters may contain harmful bacteria. Use clean water to wash wounds.
  • Stop Bleeding: Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth or bandage to control bleeding.
  • Treat Hypothermia: If someone is cold and shivering, wrap them in dry blankets and warm them gradually.
  • Disinfect Wounds: Use antiseptic solutions or clean water to prevent infections.
  • Perform CPR if Necessary: In cases of drowning, check for breathing and perform CPR if needed.
  • Seek Medical Help: Call emergency services or visit a relief center if injuries are serious.
